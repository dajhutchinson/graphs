To define a graph in a text file;

- The first line must contain all the names of the desired nodes for the graph.
     i) Only a semicolon ';' separating names (no spaces);
    ii) End line with a full stop '.'.

- Define each edge on a seperate line.
     i) Only a '->' seperates names (no spaces);
    ii) Place weight in <> after end node, no space between the two;
   iii) If it is NOT the last edge put a semicolon ';' after the weight;
    iv) Otherwise end line with a full stop '.'.

NOTE:
  Lines cannot be longer than 200 characters
